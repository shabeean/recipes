spaggheti recipe

ingredients:
1. spaghetti one glass
2.table spoon of cooking oil
3. pinch of salt

method:
1.add two glasses of water in  the cooking pot
2.add oil and salt
3.bring the mixture to boil.
4.add spaghetti and cook for 7-10 minutes
5.seave out the excess water and your spaghetti is ready to serve.

serve with your favourite accompaniment.
